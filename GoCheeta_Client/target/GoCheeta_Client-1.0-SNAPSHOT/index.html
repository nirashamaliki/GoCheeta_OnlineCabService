GoCheeta is a well reputed cab service located in Sri Lanka Island wide. It has several branches like Galle, Kandy, Nugegoda, Gampaha, Kurunegala and Jaffna. GoCheeta Cabs is the most reliable cab service with a fleet of over 5,000 vehicles. Our versatility and expertise ensure that our passengers get the best cab service in Sri Lanka. Our cab service providers are qualified, trained, and experienced to provide ultimate cab services.


How to Book?

You can get registered first to the GoCheeta cab service system. Then a registered customer can order any type of vehicle available via the website. But once the booking is made, both the pick-up and destination locations will be restricted only within one exact city, that they choose out of the given list.
